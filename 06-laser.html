Laser Cutting

For the laser cutting assignment , the task was to make a music box with the dimensions given.The making of the hinges of the box was tricky but i figured it out by refering to online tutorials. After i designed the box and seperated the 6 faces, i transferred it into autocad .I cleaned the design up abit in auto cad , before importing svg files of nordic like designs from a online website to complete the box .
